introduction

The following case study deck was put together for the Google UX Design Certificate on Courera. The UX project was based on an automatically generated design challenge prompt from sharpen.design.

The prompt:
Design an order tracking app for a car dealership.

As part of the challenge, I approached the prompt from the role of a UX designer and researcher. The challenge proved to be a great creative outlet, and an amazing exercise to practice my UX skills. Not only did the course expose me to real life scenarios, but it also taught me various design pracices used by the leaders of the industry. See the link below for the case study slide deck.
